Mixtape

  • In The Boonies

    The debut track of this mixtape, "In The Boonies" is a very classic hip hop sampling of the classic Nancy Sinatra track "Bang Bang". This is a sample any producer should be familiar with and is easily crafted into a variety of classic tracks. Here the sounds of trap, hip hop, and soul are combined to bring forth a new sound.



  • Weekend Sunrise

    The feeling of the night that doesn't end but also a day that requires sunglasses. Ever constant, this soundscape has a subtle nature which is signature of Downstate New York hip hop.



  • Night Once More

    Far away from the Boonies, music pulses late into the night. The people are powered by nothing more than the fumes from the day before. It is a city that never sleeps. A sound that breathes life into the scene.
